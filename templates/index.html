About

Audio To Sign Language Translator is a WebApp for hearing impaired people which takes in audio input from the user and displays the output in form of signs which is understandable by such people.

It is very difficult to communicate with such people . In order to do so we have designed this webapp in which a normal person can speak and convey their messages via this translator which uses Indian Sign Language (ISL).
